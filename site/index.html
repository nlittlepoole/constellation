How does it work?

80% of Americans own a Smart Phone. Each phone emits a unique frequency via WiFi that Constellation can use to generate analytics. Constellation brings the power of digital analytics to the real world, no expensive equipment needed.



Act don't React

Stop operating in the dark and let Constellation show you the way. It takes less than 5 minutes to start collecting data!

Democratize Data


Anyone in your organization can use constellation. A simple point and click UI, unlimited users, and cross platform support make Constellation accessible no matter what technology your organization uses.

Advanced Analytics & Reporting

How many people visited my store yesterday? What % of visitors at my cafe are here for the first time? How long does the average table take to serve? Constellation's Advanced Analytics Engine can answer all these and more!

Data Security & Compliance

Constellation anonymizes and encrypts data so that you never have to worry about leaking private information. Constellation ensures you are compliant with data privacy standards such as GDPR. Constellation's mobile and desktop app are open source, so any security professional or consultant can verify that your customers are safe.
